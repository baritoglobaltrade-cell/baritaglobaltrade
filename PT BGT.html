About Us

PT Barito Global Trade is a trading and agribusiness company committed to connecting Indonesia’s agricultural products with international markets. Our focus is on exporting tropical fruits, spices, and locally sourced commodities, ensuring quality, sustainability, and long-term partnerships worldwide.

Vision

To become a trusted global agribusiness and trading company representing Indonesia on the world stage.

Mission

To deliver sustainable agricultural and trade solutions through innovation, integrity, and international collaboration.

Core Values

Integrity, sustainability, and partnership are the foundations of everything we do.
